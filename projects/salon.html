Angelic Salon & Spa

Online Appointment & Service Management Platform

Angelic Salon & Spa is a web-based platform designed to simplify salon appointment scheduling and service management. Users can browse services, book appointments, and manage their profiles, while salon owners can track customers, employees, and service details through a dedicated dashboard.

Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this project focuses on a modern and responsive design that provides a smooth experience for both customers and administrators.
